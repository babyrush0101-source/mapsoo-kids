# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Mapsoo Worldsmith a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give and accept technical feedback in good faith.
- Credit art, code, research, and documentation sources accurately.
- Respect privacy, licenses, and community safety boundaries.
- Focus criticism on work and decisions, never on a person.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, sexualized attention, threats, doxxing, deliberate misuse of private data, and knowingly submitting unlicensed or harmful content are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact method on the repository owner's GitHub profile. Maintainers will respect the privacy and security of reporters.
